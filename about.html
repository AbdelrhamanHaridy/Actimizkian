About Actimizkian

Actimizkian is a company which has been working since 2016, with different departments serving different roles in the community

  • 1st Department is Engineering For Geniuses which works at education field to improve and develop the community through training, workshops, camps and competitions.
  • 2nd Department is SEED which works at providing educational and entertainment kits as Robotics, Science, Arts & Crafts, Engineering, Technological, STEAM, Space and Disabled kits for all ages starting from age 5.
  • 3rd Department is Innovation solution which works at industrial field, automation, mechanical and electrical machine, application for Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science and related applications principles. 4th Department Actimizkian Events which works at events as seminars, exhibitions or organizing any kind of events.

    • Actimizkian is an entity works to improve and develop the community through integration of the latest modern world Technology, Science and Arts to provide different types of services to make it easy and available to whom have passion, talent or want to know more.
